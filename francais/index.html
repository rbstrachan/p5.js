Welcome to Reiwa's

French Noun Gender Practice Tool!


Here's how to use it.

  • A French noun will be displayed on the screen.
  • Choose the gender you think to be correct by clicking the appropriate button. Alternatively, on your keyboard, press m and f (or 1 and 2) for masculine and feminine respectively.
  • Click the green vérifier ma réponse button or press Enter to submit your answer. Use Space to skip the current question.
  • The tool will tell you if your answer is correct or not, display feedback and update your score. A new word will then be displayed for you to practice.

Have fun! If you have any questions, you can contact reiwa in the r/French discord server.
